About Me

Hi, I'm Sadhika Huria—a passionate problem-solver and tech enthusiast. My love for mathematics and logical thinking led me to explore the world of Computer Science. Though I had no prior knowledge, I took a leap of faith, and now I'm fully immersed in learning, building, and creating.

My Journey

Mathematics was always my strength—solving problems felt like an art form to me. I enjoyed breaking down complex problems, showcasing each step as part of a bigger picture. When I heard Computer Science was related to math and critical thinking, I decided to give it a try. Now, I’m fully committed to improving my skills, mastering programming languages, and developing projects that make an impact.

Tutoring

My love for mathematics, led me to teach others this art. I wanted to teach others the thought process behind solving critical problems. I have been teaching Pre-Calculus 10 - 12, Calculus 12, Foundations of Mathematics 10 - 12 since 2021.
